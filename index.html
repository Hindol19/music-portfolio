About me

With over 13 years of dedicated experience, I am a multi-talented music producer, composer, and film score composer who has left an indelible mark on the music industry. My journey as a session musician and guitarist has enriched my skills and allowed me to collaborate with a diverse array of artists and creators.

My passion for crafting emotive soundscapes and compositions is a driving force behind my work. From the intricate subtleties of film scores to the electrifying energy of live performances, I bring a deep understanding of musical storytelling to every project.

Whether it's weaving captivating melodies or exploring innovative sonic realms, my commitment to pushing creative boundaries shines through in every note I play and produce. I look forward to joining you on your musical journey and turning your vision into an unforgettable sonic experience.
